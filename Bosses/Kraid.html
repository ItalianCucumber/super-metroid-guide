Kraid

DESCRIPTION

Kraid is a large lizard monster that is protecting the Varia Suit. To defeat Kraid you must shoot him in the mouth while avoiding
his projectiles and the spikes at the bottom of the room. When first going down the hallway, you will see a small version of Kraid,
this is a false Kraid as the real one is further down the hallway.

LOCATION

Brinstar

REWARDS

Varia Suit

Kraid

Fake Kraid
Kraid

Abilities

Can shoot spikes from belly
Can spew rock-like projectiles
