Phantoon

DESCRIPTION

Phantoon is a large squid monster that is usurping power from the ship. To defeat Phantoon you must shoot him in the eye
while avoiding the Phantom Eyes being thrown at you. If you are able to defeat it, it will fade away into the rest of the
ship.

LOCATION

Wrecked Ship

REWARDS

Gravity Suit

Power restored to the Ship

Phantoon

Phantoon

Abilities

Can attack you with Phantom Eyes
