Mother Brain

DESCRIPTION

Mother Brain is a large brain monster that has control over the planet Zebes. To defeat Mother Brain you must first shoot her
while she is in the glass tank. If you are able to defeat her then, she will seem to die off, but then come back with a large,
monstrous body. If you are able to dodge the constant attacks from her and shoot missiles at the head, you will be able to
successfully defeat her.

LOCATION

Tourian
