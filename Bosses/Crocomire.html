Crocomire

DESCRIPTION

Crocomire is a large lizard monster that is protecting the Grapple Beam. To defeat Crocomire you must shoot him in the mouth
while not letting him advance towards you and push you into the spikes. If you are able to push it back far enough, it will break
the floor and fall into lava. When first falling down the tunnel, Crocomire will not attack until you do.

LOCATION

Norfair

REWARDS

Grapple Beam

Crocomire

Crocomire

Abilities

Can push you backwards
Can attack you with claws
Can spit plasma balls
