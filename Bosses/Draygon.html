Draygon

DESCRIPTION

Draygon is a large monster that is protecting the Space Jump. To defeat Draygon you must latch on to one of the open electrical
boards while he is held onto you, while not getting attacked. If you are able to defeat it, it start to shrivel up. When first
entering the room, you are greeted with 4 enemies grouping together.

LOCATION

Maridia

REWARDS

Space Jump

Draygon

Draygon

Abilities

Can grab you and deal damage
