Zebes

DESCRIPTION

Zebes is the planet that the game takes place on. This planet has been taken over by space pirates and their leader, Ridley.
This planet is filled with harsh environments that Samus must traverse to defeat the space pirates and turn it back to the way
it was before. The regions on Zebes range from a boiling, lava-filled cave to a sunken ship with mysterious ancient technology.
