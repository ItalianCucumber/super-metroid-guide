Gravity Suit

DESCRIPTION

The Gravity Suit is an upgraded version of the Power Suit. This suit is able to withstand extreme gravitational forces,
such as underwater and in space, while also offering a reduction in damage. Once Phantoon is defeated, this suit
is then available to Samus as you traverse the rest of the game. This suit is the final suit upgrade attainable
in the game. The name originates from it's ability to explore unhindered when in environments with unusual
gravity and negates any restrictions

LOCATION

Wrecked Ship; Rewarded for defeating Phantoon

Gravity Suit

Sprite of Gravity Suit in-game

Abilities

Damage Reduction
Prevents damage from lava contact
Negates extreme gravity

and Speed Booster to work underwater
