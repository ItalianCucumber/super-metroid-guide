Power Suit

DESCRIPTION

The Power Suit is a suit of powered armour. This suit is highly customizable, offering certain modifications
such as missiles or upgrades. When beginning the game, this is the suit that Samus starts out with, as you travel
through the landscape, you find more items to improvethe abilities of the suit. This suit is able to highly improve
the capabilities of Samus herself. The suit comes with a Power Beam and Oxygen Supply Equipment
allowing her to breath near indefinitely underwater, space, or other harsh environments.
