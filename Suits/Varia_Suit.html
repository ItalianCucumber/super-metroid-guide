Varia Suit

DESCRIPTION

The Varia Suit is an upgraded version of the Power Suit. This suit is able to withstand extreme heat, while
also offering a reduction in damage. Once Kraid is defeated, this suit is then available to Samus as you traverse
the rest of the game. This suit is considered as Samus' main suit in many games. The name originates from
a mistranslation of 'Barrier Suit' which was eventually just kept and popularized.

LOCATION

Brinstar; Rewarded for defeating Kraid

Varia Suit

Sprite of Varia Suit in-game

Abilities

Damage Reduction
Protection from Extreme Heat
