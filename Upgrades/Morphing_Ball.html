Morphing Ball

DESCRIPTION

The Morphing Ball is an upgrade to the Power Suit. This upgrade is able to turn Samus into a ball, when in a ball Samus
move quicker, while also being upgradable. This upgrade is the easiest to attain as it is right at the beginning.
This is a very necessary upgrade as many puzzles rely on it. Some upgrades include the Spring Ball and bombs.
