Space Jump

DESCRIPTION

The Space Jump is an upgrade to the Power Suit. This upgrade allows Samus to indefinitely spin jump. Once Draygon
is defeated, this upgradeis then available to Samus as you traverse the rest of the game. This upgrade works with
the Screw Attack, allowing you to indefinitely spin and attack enemies.

LOCATION

Maridia; Rewarded for defeating Draygon

Space Jump

Sprite of Space Jump upgrade

Abilities

Allows Samus to indefinitely spin jump
