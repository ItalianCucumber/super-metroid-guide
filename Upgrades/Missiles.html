Missiles

DESCRIPTION

Missiles are an upgrade to the Power Suit. This upgrade is able to shoot projectiles that cause higher damage than
the normal beam. Once you collect your first missile upgrade, missiles are then freely available to Samus as you go through
the remainder of the game. There are two types of missiles inthis game, the regular Missile and the Super Missile. Certain doors
and blocks are restricted and need to be opened by Missiles of varying levels.

LOCATION

Missile expansions can be found all across the map
