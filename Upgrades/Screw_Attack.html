Screw Attack

DESCRIPTION

The Screw Attack is an upgrade to the Power Suit. This upgrade allows Samus to attack whilst spin jumping. Once the
Chozo statue is defeated, this upgrade is then available to Samus as you finish the rest of the game. This upgrade
is the final upgrade attainablein the game. The Screw Attack works well with the Space Jump allowing Samus to
spin jump attack indefinitely.

LOCATION

Norfair
