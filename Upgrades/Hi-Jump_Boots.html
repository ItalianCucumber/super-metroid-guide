Hi-Jump Boots

DESCRIPTION

The Hi-Jump Boots are an upgrade to the Power Suit. This upgrade is able to let Samus jump higher.Once you get these boots,
you are able to reach certain area you were unable to before.This is one of the upgrades that isn't necessary to finish the game.

Norfair
