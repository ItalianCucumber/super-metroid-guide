Bombs

DESCRIPTION

Bombs are an upgrade to the Power Suit. Bombs are able to stun certain enemies, can damage areas that are difficult to reach,
it can also destroy some blocks. Once you get the bomb upgrade, bombs are freely available to Samus as you go through the
game. There are two varieties of bombs, the Bomb and Power Bomb. Regular bombs are only able to explode a small area
whereas a Power Bomb can detonate an entire room, killing enemies and blowing up any blocks.

LOCATION

Bomb expansions can be found all across the map
